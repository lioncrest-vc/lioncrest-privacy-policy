Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Lioncrest Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome Extension ("Lioncrest Data Extractor") and related services. By using our extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Information

  • Google Account Information: We collect your Google account email address and profile information through Google OAuth to authenticate and authorize access to Gmail.
  • Domain Verification: We verify that your email address belongs to authorized domains (@lioncrest.vc or @prospeq.co).

2.2 Gmail Data

  • Email Content: We access and process email thread content from Gmail to extract structured data for business purposes.
  • Thread Information: We collect Gmail thread IDs and metadata necessary for data extraction functionality.

2.3 Extracted Data

  • Business Information: We process and store business-related data extracted from emails, including but not limited to contact information, deal flow data, investor information, and network details.
  • User Edits: We collect any modifications you make to extracted data through our interface.
Important: We only access Gmail data when you explicitly use our extension and only process emails that you specifically select for data extraction.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and ensure only authorized users access the service
  • Data Extraction: To process email content and extract structured business data using AI/LLM technology
  • Monday.com Integration: To save extracted data to your Monday.com boards and workspaces
  • Service Improvement: To improve our data extraction algorithms and user experience
  • Support: To provide technical support and resolve issues

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Extracted data is temporarily processed on our secure servers
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser
  • Processed data is sent to Monday.com through secure API connections

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We implement industry-standard security practices
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security measures

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API: To access and read email content with your explicit permission
  • Monday.com API: To save extracted data to your Monday.com workspace
  • OpenAI: To process and extract structured data from email content using AI technology

5.2 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • To the third-party services mentioned above as necessary for functionality
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Retention

  • Email content is processed temporarily and not permanently stored on our servers
  • Extracted data is retained only as long as necessary to complete the Monday.com integration
  • Authentication tokens are stored until you revoke access or uninstall the extension
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Revoke Access: Revoke Gmail access permissions at any time through Google Account settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension to stop all data collection

8. Domain Restrictions

Our extension is designed exclusively for use by Lioncrest Ventures and Prospeq employees. Access is restricted to email addresses from the following domains:

  • @lioncrest.vc
  • @prospeq.co

Users with email addresses from other domains will be denied access to the service.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
